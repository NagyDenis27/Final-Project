Our Story:

Over 40 years of print quality!

In Priesendorf near Bamberg, a team of around 30 well-trained and motivated employees work hand in hand in the areas of printing, finishing, processing and digital. With a state-of-the-art machine park, we produce high-quality printed matter on an area of ​​more than 2500 square meters and offer our customers in the region and throughout Germany intelligent solutions that pull out all the stops of the exciting printing trade. In April 2016 the company celebrated its 40th anniversary. Together with the managing director, experienced colleagues personally take on the training to become media technologists in print, media designers and media clerks in digital and print. In the following we are happy to provide information about the various fields of activity of the professions.

Media technologist in print

Print media technologists set up printing presses. You prepare print data and control and monitor the printing process for the production of advertising materials, newspapers, magazines, books, catalogs and other print products. Media technologist printing is a 3-year recognized apprenticeship in industry and trade.

Media technologist in print processing

Media technologists in print processing produce finished newspapers, books, calendars or brochures from preliminary products. You will plan and control the necessary manufacturing processes, set up machines and systems and take into account upstream and downstream operations.

Media clerk

Digital and print media clerks work in marketing and sales of media and services. They also plan, direct and control the production of media products.

Media designer

Digital and print media designers specializing in design and technology design media products and plan production processes. They combine media elements, prepare data for digital use and compile them for the respective purpose.
